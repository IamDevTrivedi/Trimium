## Introduction

Welcome to Trimium's Privacy Policy. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Trimium's URL shortening and link management services.

By using Trimium, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access or use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create a Trimium account, we collect:

- **Email address** - Used for account authentication, notifications, and communications
- **Username** - Used to identify your account within the platform
- **Password** - Stored securely using industry-standard hashing algorithms
- **Profile information** - Optional details you choose to provide, such as your name or organization

### Link Data

When you create short links, we collect and store:

- **Original URLs** - The destination URLs you shorten
- **Custom aliases** - Any custom short codes you create
- **Link settings** - Expiration dates, password protection, and other configurations
- **Tags and metadata** - Organizational labels you assign to links

### Analytics Data

When users click on Trimium short links, we collect:

- **Click timestamps** - When the link was accessed
- **IP addresses** - Used to derive approximate geographic location (city/country level)
- **User agent information** - Browser type, operating system, and device type
- **Referrer information** - The website or app from which the click originated
- **Geographic data** - Country, region, and city derived from IP address

### Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your use of Trimium:

- **Log data** - Pages visited, features used, and actions taken within the platform
- **Device information** - Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution
- **Session data** - Login times, session duration, and interaction patterns

### Provide Our Services

- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and malicious activity

## Cookies and Tracking

Trimium uses cookies and similar technologies to:

- **Authenticate users** - Keep you logged in across sessions
- **Remember preferences** - Store your settings and preferences
- **Analyze usage** - Understand how users interact with our platform
- **Improve performance** - Optimize loading times and functionality

### Types of Cookies We Use

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| Essential | Required for core functionality | Session |
| Authentication | Keep you logged in | 30 days |
| Preferences | Remember your settings | 1 year |
| Analytics | Understand usage patterns | 1 year |

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect your ability to use some features of Trimium.

## Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption in transit** - All data transmitted to and from Trimium uses TLS/SSL encryption
- **Encryption at rest** - Sensitive data is encrypted in our databases
- **Secure authentication** - Passwords are hashed using argon2 with appropriate cost factors
- **Access controls** - Strict internal access policies limit who can access user data
- **Regular audits** - Periodic security assessments and penetration testing
- **Monitoring** - Continuous monitoring for suspicious activity

While we strive to protect your information, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and enable available security features.

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

- **Account data** - Retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Link data** - Retained while your account is active or until you delete the links
- **Analytics data** - Aggregated and anonymized after 2 years
- **Log data** - Retained for 90 days for security and troubleshooting purposes

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion feature in your settings.
